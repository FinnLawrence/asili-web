Design thoughtfully,
tread lightly,
know the story.

ASILI is a collaborative design platform with social impact. We guide collaborations between contemporary designers and East African artisans. ASILI was established as a way to bring ideas together; heritage and present day, craft and industry. Each partnership considers the true character of a natural material within a modern design framework.
